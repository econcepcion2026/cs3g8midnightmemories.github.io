Tips and Tricks

It turns out that there are some unexpected strategies: occasionally, you might want to give up your turn by purposefully failing to make a match!

Most players use "2-moves," in which they turn over an unknown card and attempt to match it with one they have already seen. If no matches appear, you turn over a second unknown card and look for a match there.

A "1-move" is when you flip over an unknown card and attempt to match it with a card you have already seen. You turn over a known card that is not a match if you are unable to make a match. In other words, a 1-move can have up to 1 card that is unknown flipped up.
